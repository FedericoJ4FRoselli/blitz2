BitMap 0,320,256,8

InitCopList 0,44,256,$1008,8,256,0

For i=255 To 0 Step -1:AGAGreen 0,i,i,i,i:Next ; grey scale palette

BLITZ

CreateDisplay 0

DisplayBitMap 0,0
DisplayPalette 0,0

Use BitMap 0:BlockScroll 1
For i= 0 To 255
	Circle 160,128,i,i
Next
